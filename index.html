About Bleek Studios

Bleek Studios, founded by T. Fraser, is a dynamic SDMA (Software, Design, Marketing & Advertising) business based in the heart of London's financial district, Canary Wharf. With a rich background in investment banking and fintech, our founder brings a unique blend of financial acumen and technological innovation to every project. As software engineers and AI enthusiasts, we specialize in crafting cutting-edge digital solutions, from responsive web applications and static websites to robust backend systems, SaaS platforms, and mobile apps. Our international reach allows us to serve clients globally, bringing entrepreneurial spirit and technical expertise to businesses of all sizes. At Bleek Studios, we're not just building software; we're shaping the future of digital experiences.
