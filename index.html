Icarus

Icarus serves as the foundational codebase and starting point for all our web applications. This fully containerized code repository allows for rapid creation and deployment of new web apps. Utilising React.js, Ruby on Rails, and MySQL, we can have a minimum viable product (MVP) up and running in just a few days. Click here to learn more.

The Town Square

The Town Square is a platform dedicated to promoting freedom of speech in the UK. It encourages users to respond to daily questions and engage in discussions anonymously. As restrictions on free speech tighten in the UK, this platform serves as an oasis where citizens and journalists can freely express their thoughts. Click here to learn more.
